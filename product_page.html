Item description

This attractive Ercol Table and Chairs are an iconic and major addition, adding timeless style to any dining room or kitchen diner. This seminal design showcases the infamous attention to detail and build quality of the British furniture makers.

The tabletop and chair seats display a beautiful elm grain and comfortably accommodate four chairs when both sides are extended. It can also be used as a table and three chairs against a wall for small families and space-saving when one of the leaves is dropped. This can also be used as a table and two when the leaves are both folded down making this set ultra-functional. We have put this set together by introducing four Ercol All-Purpose chairs to the design of an incredibly efficient drop-leaf table. The dining suite brings a timeless fashion statement that remains incredibly desirable today.

We have stripped this beautiful set by hand before applying a coat of clear lacquer which will protect the wood for many years to come but also accentuate the beautiful wood grain. Full of charm and character and completely versatile in use or size, this set is sure to be a talking point and a great centrepiece in any kitchen or dining area.
